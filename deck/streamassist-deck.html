Problem

Creators duct-tape six tools to run one show.

Discord for talent. Whereby or Zoom for the call. OBS for the stream. Streamlabs for overlays. Manual bots for chat games. Twitch chat for the audience.

Every show is rebuilt from scratch. Every new format costs weeks. Every viewer interaction is glued on with tape.

Why now

Live content is becoming interactive — the toolchain hasn't caught up.

01 · Format
Audiences expect to play, not just watch.
Twitch Plays, IRL game shows, multi-creator collabs. The format is shifting from broadcast to participation.
Streamer collabs are the highest-engagement format on Twitch and Kick.
02 · Money
Creators monetize the audience directly.
Subs, gifted subs, tips and direct purchases have replaced ads. The money moved — the tools didn't.
Viewer-paid revenue is now the dominant creator income on Twitch & Kick.
03 · AI
New formats cost cents, not weeks.
Clip captioning, summaries, format suggestions, moderation — all become cheap layers on top of the show.
Show experimentation moves from a quarterly project to a weekly one.
